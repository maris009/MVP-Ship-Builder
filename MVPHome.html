SERVICES

Ventilation

Equipped to Fab/Install/Modify Ventilation in Fan Rooms, Vent Trunks, Main Spaces and Laundy, from Stainless Steel, Aluminum, to Galvanize. Including volumetric tests and Balancing.

Capability

We have the capability to work on; Moisture Separators, Plenums, Spools, Extensions, Expanded Metals, Work Stations, Missile Racks, State Rooms, Complete Galley, Mess Decks, Louvers, etc…

Doors/panels

Company has the skill and knowledge to Repair/Install Ellison Doors, Metal Joint doors, including replacing Closure, balancing the doors and modifying bulkhead panels.

Joint Strike Fighter

Our Team has the experience of installing the Furniture and Ventilation in the Joint Strike Fighter modifications. We are more than capable to work on project Habitability.

Drip Proof

We can support AIT with modification of ventilation Drip Proof or Water Tight.
